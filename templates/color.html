Huephoria

How It Works

  • Input & Output: pix2pix GANs take grayscale images as input and generate corresponding colorized images as output.
  • Conditional GAN: This GAN is conditioned on the input image, meaning the generator learns to add color based on the grayscale image content.
  • Training: The generator and discriminator are trained together, where the generator tries to create realistic color images, and the discriminator distinguishes between real and colorized images.
  • Loss Functions: Uses a combination of L1 loss (pixel-wise difference) and adversarial loss to ensure the generated images are both accurate and realistic.

How to Use This

  1. Upload Image: Click "Upload Image" and select your grayscale image.
  2. Colorize Image: Click "Colorize Image" to apply the colorization.
  3. Download Image: Click "Download Image" to save the colorized image to your device.
